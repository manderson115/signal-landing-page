The real problem

You already know something is off. You just can't see where.

Most independent agency CEOs are exceptional at the work. They've built strong teams, delivered real results for clients, and earned a reputation worth protecting.

But growth — consistent, scalable, strategic growth — is a different discipline. And most agencies are running it without the positioning, the systems, or the infrastructure that actually make it work.

The result isn't failure. It's a slow drag. Pitches that should be wins. Referrals that never materialize into a program. A positioning that describes what you do without communicating why a client should choose you over anyone else.

SIGNAL exists to show you where the drag is — and how much it's costing you.

  • Positioning that describes, not differentiates Most agency websites sell services. The agencies that grow sell solutions to specific problems — and prove it with outcomes.
  • Growth that depends on who you know If your pipeline lives in the founder's network, you don't have a growth strategy. You have a fragile dependency.
  • Win rates that signal a deeper issue A sub-30% close rate isn't a pitch problem. It's usually a positioning problem — and no amount of better decks will fix it.
  • Organic growth left on the table The most efficient revenue you'll ever generate is already inside your client relationships. Most agencies never systematically go after it.

What SIGNAL does

A diagnostic built specifically for how agencies grow — or don't.

SIGNAL evaluates your agency across six weighted domains — using both your self-reported inputs and an AI analysis of your website and LinkedIn presence. That combination matters: it's the difference between what you believe about your agency and what the market actually sees.

The output is a composite growth readiness score, benchmarked against independent agencies of comparable size. You'll know not just where you rank — but specifically what's holding you back and what to fix first.

Positioning is weighted at 30% of your total score. That's intentional. In our work with dozens of independent agencies, weak or generic positioning is the single most common root cause of underperformance across every other growth lever.

Common questions

What you might be wondering.

Is SIGNAL really free?
Yes. The diagnostic and your personalized report are completely free. The only ask is 12–30 minutes of your honest attention. If your score opens a conversation about working together, that's a good outcome — but there's no obligation attached to taking it.
Who is this built for?
CEOs, founding partners, and senior growth leaders at independent agencies. This is built for decision-makers who have both the authority and the appetite to act on what they find. Whether you're running a 5-person studio or a 200-person independent shop, the diagnostic is relevant.
What happens after I get my score?
Your report is delivered as a hosted page you can revisit and share with your leadership team. It ends with an invitation to book a 30-minute call with a Team Uncommon strategist to walk through a custom growth roadmap based on your specific results. That conversation is where the real work starts.
If I take the Quick Read, can I go back and complete the Full Diagnostic later?
Yes. Your Quick Read answers are saved. If you decide to go deeper — right after finishing or days later — you won't re-enter anything. You'll pick up exactly where you left off, with only the additional questions remaining.
How is my data used?
Your responses are used to generate your personalized report. If you request a growth roadmap session, your results are shared with our team to prepare for that conversation. We don't share individual agency data with third parties.
